Features

Everything you need for 3D storytelling

From model import to final render, Sintekno gives you professional-grade tools in a streamlined, intuitive interface.

Universal 3D Import

Drop in models from Blender, GLB, GLTF, OBJ, FBX and more. Sintekno handles the rest — materials, textures, and hierarchy included.

Camera Keyframing

Place your camera, set keyframes, and let Sintekno interpolate smooth cinematic paths. Dolly, pan, orbit — full control.

Object Animation

Rotate, translate, and scale any object in your scene with precise keyframe controls. Easing curves included for natural motion.

Timeline Editor

A visual, multi-track timeline to manage all your animations. Drag keyframes, adjust timing, and preview in real-time.

Scene Lighting

Add point lights, directional lights, and ambient lighting to set the mood. Real-time preview updates as you adjust.

Video Export

Render your scene to high-quality video directly in the browser. Choose resolution, frame rate, and format — no external tools needed.

Why Sintekno

Because 3D shouldn't need a studio

Traditional 3D video workflows are complex. You need multiple tools — a modeler, a scene compositor, a keyframe editor, a renderer — each with its own learning curve and price tag.

Sintekno was built with a simple belief: if you can model it in Blender, you should be able to animate and film it in your browser. No installs. No subscriptions. No compromises.

The Creator

Meet Adam Khayari

Adam Khayari is a passionate developer with a deep interest in 3D graphics, creative tools, and making complex technology accessible to everyone. Sintekno was born from his frustration with the fragmented 3D video production pipeline.

"I wanted to build something that lets anyone take a 3D model and turn it into a video — right in the browser, without installing gigabytes of software. Sintekno is that tool."
